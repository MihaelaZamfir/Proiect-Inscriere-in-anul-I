Regie
General Presentation
The Regie campus, is the largest student dormitory in Bucharest, and consists of 33 buildings, of which 6 belong to the University of Bucharest(called U’s) and the rest belong to the Politehnica University (called P’s).
The Regie campus is regarded more like a neighborhood than a student campus everywhere it is mentioned. The Regie campus is located in the western part of Bucharest, along the Dâmboviţa River starting from the Basarab overpass (Orhideea) as far as the Petrache Poenaru metro station, somewhere between the Politehnica University of Bucharest and Crângaşi neighborhood. It is an area that is not so crowded, whit a pretty intense nightlife, that is not so noisy, that also contributed to the development of the student life. Cut a long story short it has everything for everyone: pubs, fast-food, restaurants, clubs, cafes, police department, ATM’s, medical  center, barber shop and supermarkets.
Dormitories:

(POZA)

There are 27  dormitories that belong to the Politehnica University and another 6 (U’s from U1 to U6) that belong to the Faculty of Medicine Carol Davila. They do not have a specific alignment (strategically speaking) so they are scattered throughout the campus. Each dormitory has 5 levels, no elevator, they are thermally insulated and painted with 2 colors(pink and brick red) and double glazed windows in each room.
P1 to P11 dormitories have rooms with 4 beds and a bathroom. Also they have a small hallway when entering the room. 
P13, P14, P17 and P18 dormitory’s have 4 beds, the same as the above except that the bathroom is down the hall.
The  dormitories P15,P16 and from P21 to P27 have 2 beds and the bathroom down the hall.
The  dormitories P12 and P19 are sort of an exception because they are intended for foreign students and teachers that come from other areas of the country. The rooms have 2 beds and a bathroom. P19 also has an air conditioning unit in every room.
All the dormitories have also a security point where are usually 1, 2 guards or in some cases even more to ensure the peace and security of the students. You can also stay in the  dormitories during the summer time(if you are a student of course) with a fee of 10 LEI per day. The dormitories are paid behind the P6 dormitory.
In the Regie campus there are 3 small shops with an average diversity of products, what I mean is that you can find something that you need. At some articles the prices are almost double of what you think a normal price should be. One of the stores is Florady that is behind the p7 dormitory, anther one is near P17 and the 3rd is in the passageway from P4. All the shops are open non-stop.
